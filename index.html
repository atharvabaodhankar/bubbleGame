Welcome to Hit the Mark,
a fast-paced bubble popping challenge! Get ready to test your reflexes and accuracy in this exciting game.



Here's how to play:
  1. You have 60 seconds to score as many points as possible.
  2. Look at the top of the screen! You'll see a special box called the Hit Box.
  3. The Hit Box will display a single number.
  4. The bubbles filled with numbers will float across the screen.
  5. Click only on the bubbles that have the same number as displayed in the Hit Box.
  6. Each successful click scores you a +1 point.
  7. Clicking on any bubble with a different number won't give you points, so be quick and precise!
  8. The faster you pop the matching bubbles, the higher your score will climb! Can you stay focused and keep up with the ever-changing Hit Box? Remember, accuracy is key! Good luck, and get ready to pop your way to a high score!
